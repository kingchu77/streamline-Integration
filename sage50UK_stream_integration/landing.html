Overview

Integrate Sage 50 with Stream and Zynk to automate the full journey of your sales orders and stock movements. Orders created in Sage 50 are pushed to Stream via the API, enabling transport planning without delays. Stream's delivery updates then flow back into Sage 50, so finance and customer service always see the latest status.

By combining Sage 50, Stream, and Zynk automation, you cut errors, save time, keep stock levels aligned across locations, and deliver reliable customer updates—without compromising security.
