Single Stock Location (Starter)

Overview

Build a live, single or multi-warehouse stock system with Sage 50, Stream, and Zynk automation. Secure APIs move orders between Sage 50 and Stream, and stock movements stay in sync without manual entry.

What you get

Live stock movements

Stock updates automatically as goods move in or out of the warehouse—no rekeying.

Stream to Sage POs

Purchase orders raised in Stream are received into Sage 50 automatically.

Stock alerts

Scheduled alerts to administrators when levels drop or thresholds hit.

Auto-PO placement

Optional automatic placement of purchase orders to keep replenishment flowing.

Goods-In setup

Stream methods for goods-in plus visibility of POs, warehouse actions, and GRNs.

Admin time saved

Reduce manual updates across Sage and Stream; keep data clean and audit-ready.

Multiple warehouse capabilities

Location-aware product codes

Set up Sage 50 product codes by department or site so each warehouse tracks stock cleanly.

Stock transfers

Enable controlled stock transfers between warehouses with clear movements recorded in Sage 50.

Cross-depot accuracy

Maintain correct levels even when orders are placed from one depot and fulfilled from another.
